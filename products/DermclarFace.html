DESCRIPTION
Dermclar Face

Dermclar Face is a premixed cocktail of active ingredients providing nutrient rich, revitalising agents that rejuvenate the skin, regenerate and restore skin luminosity.

  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkle depth
  • Brightens dull skin, restoring skin radiance
  • Firms, lifts and tightens the skin
  • Protects the skin, counteracting harmful environmental factors

Vitamin C antioxidant essential for collagen synthesis, providing tissue strength and radiance.

DMAE is the precursor of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter involved in muscle contraction. It protects from the damages induced by free radicals and stimulates, thanks to its vasodilatory action, blood supply to the tissues.

Argireline (Hexapeptide 8) pentapeptide mimics the N-terminal end of SNAP-25 and competes with this natural protein for a position in the SNARE complex. As a result, neurotransmitters are not efficiently released, facial contractions are weakened and muscles relaxed, thus inhibiting the formation of expression line and wrinkles.

Leuphasyl , pentapeptide, couples to the enkephalin receptor and closes the calcium channels and so inhibits the release of acetylcholine in the synapse, reducing muscle contraction.

Hyaluronic Acid is widely distributed throughout the body and fills the space between collagen and elastin fibres. It provides a cohesion between the connective tissue that encourages essential nutrients to be transported from the bloodstream to the cells. This results in a slowing down of the aging process, stimulating fibroblast function and collagen synthesis, and helping to eliminate harmful compounds from the skin.
Hyaluronic acid has the ability to bind to and retain water, a thousand times its own molecular weight, thereby keeping skin hydrated, adding volume and elasticity.

Organic Silica is the most important active ingredient present in nature that promotes the synthesis of collagen and elastin, stabilizes and maintains the architecture of tissues, stimulating fibroblasts and promoting the biosynthesis of collage fibres, elastin and proteoglycans.

Oligoelements essential for the correct functioning of numerous biological processes.

Glutathione is a potent antioxidant, brightens the skin and strengthens the immune system.
