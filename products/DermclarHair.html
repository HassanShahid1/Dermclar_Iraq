Dermclar Hair

Dermclar Hair is a cocktail of nutrients that counter hair and scalp deficiencies, encouraging strong and healthy hair growth. It stimulates cell activity, improving microcirculation and metabolism. This enables effective protein synthesis whilst enhancing resistance to environmental and physical damage.

The nutrients penetrate the hair root, creating a protective film that keeps hair hydrated whilst antioxidants protect the hair.

  • Stimulates hair growth and reduces hair loss
  • Improves shine
  • Hydrates the scalp and hair
  • Detoxifies

Organic Silica is the most important active ingredient present in nature that promotes the synthesis of collagen and elastin, stabilizes and maintains the architecture of tissues, stimulating fibroblasts and promoting the biosynthesis of collage fibres, elastin and proteoglycans. Organic Silica alkalizes the cells, allowing the hair cells to maintain a healthy pH and helps balance trace minerals essential for a healthy scalp and hair.

Rutin protects veins and strengthens capillaries.

Hyaluronic Acid is widely distributed throughout the body and fills the space between collagen and elastin fibres. It provides a cohesion between the connective tissue that encourages essential nutrients to be transported from the bloodstream to the cells. This results in a slowing down of the aging process, stimulating fibroblast function and collagen synthesis, and helping to eliminate harmful compounds from the skin.

Hyaluronic acid has the ability to bind to and retain water, a thousand times its own molecular weight, thereby keeping skin hydrated, adding volume and elasticity.

Oligoelements essential for the correct functioning of numerous biological processes.

D-Panthenol supports numerous enzymatic reactions and metabolic functions of the cell, revitalising and conditioning hair.

Amino-Acids providing the building blocks for efficient protein synthesis.
