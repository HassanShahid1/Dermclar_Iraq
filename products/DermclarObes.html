Dermclar Obes

Dermclar Obes is a premixed cocktail of fat burning and draining ingredients which reduce localised adiposity deposits from any area of the body. It helps to restore body balance and contour the body shape.

REDUCING ACTIONS

  • Reduces localised adiposity
  • Effective in reducing the appearance of cellulite
  • Improves skin tone and texture

Phosphatidylcholine (PPC) plays an important role in intra and extracellular metabolic transport. It induces an enzymatic reaction that aids in the dissolution of fat which is metabolised in the liver by beta-oxidation. PPC plays an important function in lipid homeostasis, helping to increase cholesterol transport and inhibit cholesterol build up in the tissues.

L-Carnitine is the transporter of fatty acids across the mitochondrial barrier, diverting activated fatty acids towards beta-oxidation and are therefore not used by the body to form new triglycerides.

Rutin reduces venous oedema and strengthens capillaries with an anti-inflammatory effect.

Sodium Deoxycholate leads to adipocyte death. The destroyed fat cells are then slowly removed by the macrophages in the concomitant inflammatory response, resulting in fat reduction in the treated area, while at the same time newly laid down collagen tightens the overlying skin.

Oligoelements essential for the correct functioning of numerous biological processes.
