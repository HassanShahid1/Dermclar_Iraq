DESCRIPTION
Dermclar PlasmaVit

Dermclar PlasmaVit is especially indicated for tired and dull skin, with signs of aging and/or photo-aging.

Its combination of Amino Acids, Organic Silicon and Vitamin C are combined to revitalize and brighten the skin, strengthening and deeply hydrating the tissue to give it radiance and firmness.

  • Increases the self-repair capacity of the cells
  • Compacts, thickens and firms the skin
  • Deeply hydrates
  • Improves the tone and luminosity of skin
  • Reduces fine wrinkles and expression lines

Methylsilanol Mannuronate (Organic Silica) is the most important active ingredient present in nature that promotes the synthesis of collagen and elastin, stabilizes and maintains the architecture of tissues, stimulating fibroblasts and promoting the biosynthesis of collagen fibres, elastin and proteoglycans.

Amino Acids combine to form proteins for tissue repair.

Vitamin C (ascorbic acid) is essential for collagen biosynthesis and serves as a cofactor for prolyl and lysyl hydroxylase, key enzymes that cross-link and stabilize collagen fibres. It also directly activates the transcription factors involved in collagen synthesis. Vitamin C also plays a role as an anti-pigmentation agent, decreasing melanin formation and is a potent antioxidant, eliminating activated oxygen (free radicals) and preventing (oxidation of cells) oxidative stress.
