DESCRIPTION
Liposome Cellulite

Cellulite is primarily a condition of poor microcirculation that damages underlying fat tissue.

Liposomes Cellulite performs an effective action on both the lipolytic and venotonic aspects of cellulite affected tissue, utilising classic anticelulitic extracts encapsulated in pro-liposome technology, that ensures effective penetration through the epidermis and diffusion in the tissue.

FOUR CELLULITE REDUCING ACTIONS
  • Traditional lipolytic action provided by classic anti-cellulite extracts with proven efficacy
  • Activation of microcirculation due to powerful venotonic profile of several actives
  • Revolutionary cellulite prevention due to specific adipocyte maturation
  • GHK peptide acts as a scavenger of lipid peroxidation by-products

Caffeine stimulates microcirculation, lipase and the metabolism of free fatty acids.

Ruscus Aculeatus Root Extract stimulates microcirculation, decreasing capillary permeability, reduces oedema and acts as an anti-inflammatory.

TEA-Hydroiodidestimulates lipase activities and restores normal morphology and function, acts rapidly and efficiently on the adipose tissue.

Organic Silica stabilizes and maintains the architecture of tissues, stimulating fibroblasts and promoting the biosynthesis of collagen fibres, elastin and proteoglycans.

Hedera Helix Extract (Ivy) improves venous and lymphatic drainage and aids drainage of the infiltrated tissue.

L-Carnitine reduces the formation of triglyceride and enhances the lipolytic effect of other ingredients.

Escin modifies vascular permeability by reducing the number and the diameter of the small pores of the capillary walls.

Tripeptide-1 is made up of 3 amino acids: glycine, histidine and lysine, stabilized with a copper atom. It scavenges specific by-products of lipid peroxidation, therefore strengthening cell membranes, and enhances the efficacy of other lipolytic ingredients.

Carrageenan (Chondrus Crispus) is high in minerals with water-binding properties, increasing hydration and skin suppleness.
