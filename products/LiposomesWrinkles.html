Liposomes Wrinkles

Liposomes Wrinkles contains two of the most powerful anti-aging ingredients to combat lines and wrinkles caused by facial muscle contraction, along with hyaluronic acid.

Using pro-liposome technology, encapsulating the ingredients, these special liposomes ensure effective penetration, transporting the actives through the epidermis where they diffuse deeply into the tissue.

ANTI-WRINKLE ACTIONS
  • Reduces muscular contractions that contribute to the formation of expression lines and wrinkles particularly effective for the upper third of the face area (crow's feet, forehead and eyebrows)
  • Deeply hydrates and firms the skin
  • Offers a natural looking result preserving facial expressivity

Hexapeptide 8 (Argireline), pentapeptide, mimics the N-terminal end of SNAP-25 and competes with this natural protein for a position in the SNARE complex. As a result, neurotransmitters are not efficiently released, facial contractions are weakened and muscles relaxed, thus inhibiting the formation of expression line and wrinkles.

Leuphasyl, pentapeptide, couples to the enkephalin receptor and closes the calcium channels and so inhibits the release of acetylcholine in the synapse, reducing muscle contraction.

Hyaluronic Acidstimulates fibroblast function and collagen synthesis, hydrates the skin, adding volume, elasticity and smoothing out wrinkles.

INDICATIONS
Facial expression lines and wrinkles

The upper third of the face area (crow's feet, forehead and eyebrows).
